About Me

Hi, I'm Kyler Cecil — the photographer behind K. Cecil Media.

Based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, I specialize in capturing the excitement of high school and motorsports events, as well as the natural beauty of landscapes and wildlife. My goal is to freeze moments that tell a story — whether it's the intensity of a football game, the peaceful stillness of a morning field, or the adrenaline of a racetrack.

I’m continually growing my portfolio and expanding into new areas of photography. I created this site to showcase my work, book clients, and publish stories that support my passion for visual storytelling.

Thanks for visiting. Feel free to reach out with questions, booking inquiries, or collaboration ideas!
